Movies I'd recommend you watch

Let's look at my top 5 rated movies

Inception (2010) – Sci-Fi/Thriller

A mind-bending film directed by Christopher Nolan that explores dreams within dreams. It has a unique concept, stunning visuals, and a compelling performance by Leonardo DiCaprio. The story keeps you thinking long after it ends.

Shawshank Redemption (1994) – Drama

A powerful story of hope, friendship, and perseverance, following a wrongly convicted man serving a life sentence. The storytelling, performances (especially by Morgan Freeman), and emotional depth make it a masterpiece.

The Dark Knight (2008) – Action/Crime

Arguably the best superhero movie ever, thanks to Heath Ledger’s unforgettable performance as the Joker. It’s intense, well-written, and packed with action while exploring deeper themes of justice and chaos.

Parasite (2019) – Thriller/Dark Comedy

A brilliantly crafted film about class struggle, deception, and survival. The twists and turns keep you engaged, and the social commentary is sharp and thought-provoking. It won the Oscar for Best Picture for a reason.

Spirited Away (2001) – Animated/Fantasy

A stunningly beautiful Studio Ghibli film with a deep, magical story about self-discovery and resilience. The animation is breathtaking, and the characters are unforgettable.
